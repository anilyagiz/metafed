MetaFed: Advancing Privacy, Performance, and Sustainability in Federated Metaverse Systems

Muhammet Anil Yagiz, Zeynep Sude Cengiz, Polat Goktas
arXiv:2508.17341v1 [cs.LG]
2024
Paper Code arXiv

Abstract

The rapid evolution of metaverse technologies has created unprecedented demands for scalable, privacy-preserving machine learning solutions. Traditional centralized approaches face significant challenges in handling the massive scale, diverse data types, and stringent privacy requirements of metaverse applications. This paper introduces MetaFed, a comprehensive federated learning framework specifically designed for metaverse systems that addresses three critical dimensions: privacy, performance, and sustainability.

MetaFed employs advanced cryptographic techniques including homomorphic encryption and secure multi-party computation to ensure robust privacy preservation while enabling efficient collaborative learning across distributed metaverse nodes. Our framework incorporates novel optimization algorithms that reduce communication overhead by up to 60% compared to traditional federated learning approaches, while maintaining model accuracy through innovative aggregation strategies.

Furthermore, MetaFed integrates blockchain-based incentive mechanisms and energy-efficient computation protocols to promote sustainable participation in federated learning processes. Extensive evaluations on real-world metaverse datasets demonstrate that our approach achieves superior performance in terms of model convergence speed, privacy guarantees, and energy consumption, making it a practical solution for large-scale metaverse deployments.

Key Contributions

Privacy-Preserving Framework: MetaFed implements advanced cryptographic techniques including homomorphic encryption and secure multi-party computation to ensure data privacy while enabling collaborative learning across metaverse nodes.

Performance Optimization: Our novel aggregation algorithms and communication protocols reduce overhead by up to 60% compared to traditional federated learning approaches, significantly improving convergence speed and model accuracy.

Sustainability Integration: The framework incorporates blockchain-based incentive mechanisms and energy-efficient computation protocols, promoting sustainable participation in federated learning processes with reduced carbon footprint.

Metaverse-Specific Design: Unlike generic federated learning solutions, MetaFed is specifically architected for metaverse environments, handling diverse data types, real-time requirements, and massive scale deployments.

Technical Innovation

Federated Architecture: MetaFed employs a distributed architecture with federated agents that coordinate learning across multiple metaverse nodes while maintaining data locality and privacy.

Carbon-Aware Scheduling: The system incorporates intelligent resource scheduling that considers energy consumption and carbon emissions, optimizing for both performance and environmental sustainability.

Advanced Aggregation: Novel aggregation strategies ensure model convergence while handling heterogeneous data distributions common in metaverse environments.

Blockchain Integration: Incentive mechanisms built on blockchain technology encourage participation while maintaining transparency and trust in the federated learning process.

Experimental Results

Performance Evaluation: Extensive experiments on MNIST and CIFAR-10 datasets demonstrate superior convergence properties compared to baseline federated learning approaches.

Privacy Guarantees: Theoretical and empirical analysis confirms robust privacy preservation through cryptographic techniques while maintaining model utility.

Sustainability Metrics: MetaFed achieves significant reductions in CO2 emissions per training round while maintaining competitive accuracy levels.

Scalability Analysis: The framework demonstrates excellent scalability properties suitable for large-scale metaverse deployments with thousands of participating nodes.
